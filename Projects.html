Shadnagar History

Is located at Hyderabad to Bangalore National highway (NH-44), Just 20 mins drive from Shamshabad International Airport. At present Hyderabad city growth is extending to sub-urban areas and so Shadnagar Town became a prominent one. The state government has recently sanctioned Rs. 100 crore to Shadnagar municipality in order to undertake the development activities.

The state government has allotted 158.69 acres of land for Star Hotels, 316 acres of land for National Remote Sensing Agency (NRSA) and 800 Acres of land for Zoo Park & Amusement park. Established housing constructions like DLF are looking forward to construct houses near by Shadnagar. Procter & Gamble, Air-Cargo, NATCO Pharma, Textile Parks, IT Parks, Hardware Parks, SEZ’s and many more industries are located near Shadnagar.
